Last updated: 23 June 2026

Purpose: one-page copy/paste draft for the first real controlled quote-review lead. Use this after completing the manual worksheet and before any paid quote-review, PDF automation, customer account or CRM workflow exists.

Deployment status: not needed. This is a local operator template only.

Do not present this as legal advice, compliance approval, HBC approval, strata approval, building certification, guaranteed savings, a final quote or a decision that another contractor's quote is approved or rejected.

### General review prompts

These are general review prompts only and are not legal advice, compliance approval or building certification.

- written contract review may be required for residential building work over $5,000 including GST
- deposit guidance should be reviewed, including 10% maximum deposit guidance
- HBC review may be required for projects over $20,000 including GST
- licensed plumbing, electrical and gas trades require review where relevant
- strata/apartment work may require owners corporation review and access coordination
- engineered-stone restrictions may affect benchtop or splashback options
- older properties may require asbestos or hazardous-material review before demolition
- final site measure is required before project-specific pricing and written scope confirmation
