# SEO Controlled Rollout Backlog

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Purpose: sequence future SEO work without creating doorway pages, duplicate thin content or unsafe quote claims before operational readiness.

Deployment status: not needed. This is a local backlog only.

## Rollout Rule

Do not expand aggressively until controlled testing proves:

- request-review leads are recoverable
- `/admin/leads` is usable
- quote-review positioning is understood
- site-measure/written-scope boundaries are clear
- no internal fields leak

## Priority Order

- `/why-kitchen-quotes-vary`
- `/kitchen-quote-sydney`
- `/kitchen-quote-review`

Goal: support quote-review conversion.

### 2. Existing Materials Pages

Goal: support access, strata and site-measure conversion.

### 4. Area Page Quality

Before creating new suburb pages, confirm:

- unique local context
- housing type specificity
- apartment/strata relevance
- older-home/access risk where relevant
- no doorway-page pattern
- clear CTA to estimate, quote review or request review

### 5. Future New Pages

Only after controlled traffic stabilises:

- comparison pages
- deeper materials pages
- suburb pages with genuine local specificity
- paid quote-review service content

## SEO Safety Rules

Every page must avoid:

- fake completed-project proof
- final fixed quote claims
- guaranteed savings claims
- legal advice claims
- compliance approval claims
- keyword stuffing
- doorway suburb duplication

Every page should include:

- one clear H1
- customer-safe explanation
- site measure / written scope wording
- internal links to `/quote`, `/quote/review`, `/request-review` or `/site-measure`
- related guide links where useful
