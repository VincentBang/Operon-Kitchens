- Message: We have a kitchen quote and want to understand whether the inclusions are clear before comparing totals.

## Sample Quote Notes

The customer describes a kitchen quote that includes cabinetry, benchtop allowance and installation, but the following details are unclear:

- demolition
- rubbish removal
- electrical relocation
- plumbing reconnection
- splashback
- appliance supply or PC allowance
- strata access and lift booking
- deposit and HBC review prompts

## Manual Trial Output

### Review Readiness

Label: `Basic review ready`

Reason: The customer has a quote and supporting context, but key inclusions and allowance details need clarification before totals can be compared.

### Scope Clarity

Medium.

The core cabinetry and benchtop work appears described, but demolition, splashback, electrical, plumbing, appliance and rubbish-removal scope need written confirmation.

### Allowance Risk

High.

Appliance and benchtop allowances may affect the planning range if products, cut-outs, joins, edge details or installation conditions are not confirmed.

### Missing Information

- Is demolition included?
- Is rubbish removal included?
- Are appliances supplied, owner-supplied or PC sums?
- Is electrical relocation included?
- Is plumbing reconnection included?
- Is splashback included?
- Are strata access, lift booking and work-hour rules allowed for?

### Customer Questions

1. Please confirm whether demolition and rubbish removal are included in the quoted total.
2. Please confirm whether appliances are included, owner-supplied or listed as PC sums.
3. Please confirm whether electrical relocation and plumbing reconnection are included or excluded.
4. Please confirm whether splashback supply and installation are included.
5. Please confirm access assumptions for lift booking, parking and strata work hours.
6. Please confirm deposit, written contract and HBC review prompts where relevant to project value.

### Compliance Prompts

These are general review prompts only:

- apartment and strata work may require owners corporation approval and access coordination
- plumbing, electrical and gas work may require licensed trade review
- residential building work may require written contract review depending on value
- HBC review may be relevant depending on project value
- final site measure and written scope confirmation are required before project-specific pricing

### Recommended Next Step

Request written clarification on the missing inclusions and allowances before comparing headline totals. If the customer wants to proceed, use a site measure to confirm access, measurements, services, selections and written scope.

## Customer-Safe Closing

This sample is general quote-clarity guidance only. It is not legal advice, compliance approval, HBC approval, strata approval, building certification or a final quote.
