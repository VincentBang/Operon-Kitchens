# Paid Review Launch Blockers

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Purpose: list what must be resolved before launching paid detailed quote review.

Deployment status: not needed.

## Blockers Before Payment

- domain and branded email sender ready
- privacy and terms reviewed for paid service wording
- price, inclusions and exclusions approved
- cancellation/refund principles approved
- manual trial reports completed and reviewed
- report template refined from real controlled leads
- delivery timing confirmed operationally
- customer file access/download rules decided
- retention policy decided
- admin lead handling workflow stable
- payment provider selected and approved
- payment task explicitly approved by Vincent

## Must Not Launch Paid Review If

- report still feels like generic advice
- site measure boundary is unclear
- customers may mistake review for legal/compliance approval
- file download/deletion/retention expectations are unclear
- email/domain still looks unprofessional
- admin workflow depends on manually checking Supabase console

## Safe Launch Principle

Paid review should sell quote clarity, not certainty.

It may help identify unclear inclusions, exclusions, PC sums, provisional sums, allowances and next questions. It must not approve a quote, certify compliance or promise savings.
