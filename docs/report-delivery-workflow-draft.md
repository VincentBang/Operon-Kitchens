# Report Delivery Workflow Draft

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Purpose: outline a future report delivery workflow without building payment, PDF automation, customer accounts or CRM.

Deployment status: not needed.

## Manual Trial Workflow

1. Customer submits `/request-review`.
2. Lead appears in `/admin/leads`.
3. Operator checks uploaded file metadata.
4. Operator downloads files only if signed-download release is approved and live.
5. Operator fills the manual quote-review worksheet.
6. Operator creates a customer-safe written summary.
7. Operator sends summary manually through approved communication channel.
8. Operator updates lead status/internal notes.

## Future Paid Workflow

Only after approval:

1. Customer purchases detailed review.
2. Customer submits quote/photos/plans.
3. Operator confirms inputs are sufficient.
4. Report is prepared using approved template.
5. Report is delivered by email or future customer portal.
6. Lead is updated in admin/CRM.

## Delivery Copy Principles

Use:

- delivered after review of supplied documents
- may require additional information
- general guidance only
- site measure and written scope confirmation required

Avoid:

- instant approval
- legally checked
- compliance certified
- final price
- guaranteed savings

## Deferred Implementation

- PDF generation
- customer portal
