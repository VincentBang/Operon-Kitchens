# Trusted Tester Instructions

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Purpose: guide trusted testers through controlled Operon Kitchens trials without implying a public launch.

Deployment status: not needed. This is a manual testing script.

## Who This Is For

Use with 2-3 trusted people who understand they are testing a controlled MVP.

## What To Ask Testers To Do

Ask each tester to try one path:

1. Start a kitchen planning estimate.
2. Review an existing kitchen quote.
3. Submit a request-review enquiry.

Optional UTM test link:

```text
/request-review?utm_source=trusted_tester&utm_medium=manual&utm_campaign=controlled_testing
```

## What To Tell Testers

Use this wording:

```text
This is a controlled test of Operon Kitchens, a Sydney kitchen quote clarity and review platform. Please treat the estimate as planning guidance only. It is not a final quote, legal advice or approval. Site measure and written scope confirmation are required before project-specific pricing.
```

## What Feedback To Capture

Ask:

- What page did you start on?
- Was it clear what Operon Kitchens does?
- Did the estimate/review/request path make sense?
- Was any wording confusing or too technical?
- Did anything feel too long on mobile?
- Did you understand that pricing is not final before site measure?
- Did you know what the next step was?

## Do Not Ask Testers To Do

- do not submit real payment details
- do not upload private documents unless they are comfortable doing so
- do not expect final pricing
- do not expect legal, HBC, strata or compliance approval
- do not treat the system as a full ordering flow
